Live Design on Twitch

What's Twitch?

Twitch (twitch.tv) is an online platform for interactive live streaming. Made popular by gaming streamers, you can also find a variety of creative streamers doing everything from coding and painting, to writing and cooking. Whether you prefer to just watch or listen (known as lurking), or chat directly with creators and entertainers, there's a community waiting for you on Twitch.

Adam streams design at twitch.tv/ux_adam

Schedule

Adam's regular schedule is Tuesday + Thursday starting at 6pm Pacific Time. For schedule changes or updates, follow @ux_adam on Twitter.

Rewards

You earn raffle tickets when you join a live stream, which you can spend on rewards like
