About Adam

Adam Ruf is a leading user experience designer who helps teams build products and features that empower people to work smarter, not harder. He's currently a UI/UX Designer on the Discover & Watch team at Twitch, a public speaker, and popular design mentor.

Adam has delivered design work for and consulted with dozens of organizations, including Yahoo, CapitalOne, UserTesting, Picaboo, Jhana, Sensilk, and many more. He taught courses on both UX research & design and web development at the Academy of Art University. Adam has also presented talks and workshops to hundreds of designers at places like Tradecraft, General Assembly, UXNight, and the Ideate Conference.

Most importantly, Adam provides training and mentorship for startups and the next generation of designers, helping to develop critical design skills, hard-working portfolios, and design-driven cultures. Through talks, workshops, and 1:1 mentoring, he's a passionate investor in the local design community.

Adam feels lucky to live in the San Francisco Bay Area with his wife, where they spend their free time discovering the world, learning new languages, laughing together, and practicing fun and challenging hobbies.
