BrightRoll Random Lunch Inviterator

For BrightRoll's HAX XV, to encourage the meeting of minds and mixing of ideas, I designed a fun way to invite a random colleague to work, a Random Lunch Inviterator.

To help Japanese language learners practice vocabulary in a new way, I designed an MVP for Mainichi Japanese. Psychology research guided us to experiment with a lower-than-ideal contrast in order help learners better memorize the characters. What do you think? Check it out now.
