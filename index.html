What I'm working on these days

New Job: Product Designer at AngelList

After a brief job hunt, I'm excited to announce I've joined AngelList. I'll be working on products and features to help grow fundraising while improving the experience across both sides of the marketplace.

Fun Fact: The organization structure at AngelList is flat. For example, my team doesn't have any product managers. Instead, designers and developers are empowered to propose, research, design, and build features and products to achieve quarterly goals.

If you have any questions about what it's like working at AngelList, feel welcome to ask me on Twitter or in live chat on Twitch.
