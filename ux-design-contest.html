How does UX Design Contest work?

  1. UX Design Contest theme is announced
  2. We swarm on a doc to define the contest details
  3. Official contest details are posted here and on Twitch
  4. ux_adam will stream his own process and progress live on Twitch
  5. Follow the requirements and submit your entry by the deadline
  6. Your entry will be reviewed live on stream
  7. Contest winners will be announced live on stream, winners must be present to claim a prize
  8. Winning entries will be showcased on this site
