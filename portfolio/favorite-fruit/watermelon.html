Origin

Watermelons are believed to have originated in southern Africa, where they still grow in the wild.

Nutrition

Watermelons are around 6% sugar and 91% water by weight. Watermelon flesh is a source of vitamin C and the red variety is a significant source of lycopene.

Watermelon Fans

China is by far the biggest producer of watermelons in the world. In 2011, the P.R.C. produced an estimated 152 billion pounds of my favorite fruit. Brazil, Iran, Turkey, and the U.S.A. round out the top five watermelon producers.
